Outlets And Shop-In-Shop

Haya Brand Stores and Shop Inshops were created with the goal of ensuring that Haya Brand reaches consumers in the same way all over the world. Therefore, our shop designer will create your store model when you design your store. It contributes to precise planning and polished presentation. The store’s front will be set up such that it is simple to search. This raises awareness of the Haya brand and makes it more visible. For all of its stores, Haya has created a very friendly and open exterior. It is one of the ways to quickly identify our stores.

The store’s outstanding navigational design makes it easy for customers to enter and browse the jewellery. In order to highlight the beauty of the jewellery and aesthetically draw buyers, displays are employed in the showroom. The furniture at Haya Store is all made by skilled craftsmen to withstand years of use without becoming damaged. The same construction model is used for all Haya stores. This is to guarantee that all Haya stores around the world meet the same quality requirements. However, it is also made sure that regional and legal customs are upheld.
